# Authentication
 
- **authentication** : 신원 확인(verifing identity) 401 Unathorized  
- **authorization** : 허가 확인(verifiing permissions) - 403 Forbidden  
- **stateful** : cookie를 이용한 sesison
- **stateless** : 토큰 기반(JWT, OAuth, ...)
